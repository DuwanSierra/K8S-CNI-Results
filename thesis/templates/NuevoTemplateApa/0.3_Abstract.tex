\chapter{Abstract}

\begin{itemize}
    \item Maximum length: 300 words per language
    \item Must include: problem, methodology, main results, conclusion
    
\end{itemize}

The abstract includes the purpose of the work and a brief explanation of the methodology used, results, and conclusions. It must clearly and concisely present the essence and importance of the work carried out. It is written in the past tense, does not include bibliographic references, and is usually the last part to be written, once the scope and contributions of the thesis are clear.\\
